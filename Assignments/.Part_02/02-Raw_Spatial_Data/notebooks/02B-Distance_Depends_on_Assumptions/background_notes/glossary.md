**Distance**  
A quantitative measure of separation based on a model of space and movement.

**Euclidean Distance**  
Straight-line distance assuming flat space and unrestricted movement.

**Path-Based Distance**  
Distance measured along a sequence of connected segments.

**Manhattan Distance**  
Distance measured using only horizontal and vertical movement.

**Assumption**  
An implicit rule or belief built into a model or calculation.

**Silent Failure**  
When a calculation produces an output that looks reasonable but is incorrect.

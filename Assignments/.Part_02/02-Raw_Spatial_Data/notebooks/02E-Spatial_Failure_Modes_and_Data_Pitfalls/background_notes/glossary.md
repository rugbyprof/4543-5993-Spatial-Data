**Failure Mode**  
A way in which spatial data or analysis produces incorrect results.

**Silent Failure**  
An error that produces plausible output without visible warnings.

**Latitude / Longitude Swap**  
An error where coordinate order is reversed, often placing data in incorrect locations.

**Invalid Coordinate**  
A numeric coordinate that falls outside acceptable ranges.

**Extent Mismatch**  
When data exists outside the current view or bounding area.

**Plausible Error**  
An incorrect result that appears reasonable at first glance.

**Sanity Check**  
A quick validation step used to confirm results make sense.

**Spatial Skepticism**  
The habit of questioning spatial outputs before accepting them as correct.

# Exam Prompts — Spatial Thinking Synthesis

## Short Answer

- What does it mean to think spatially?
- Why is spatial thinking independent of software?

## Conceptual

- How do assumptions affect spatial reasoning?
- Why is validation a core spatial skill?

## Scenario-Based

- Describe how scale, resolution, and assumptions interact in a dataset.
- Explain how editing spatial data can both clarify and mislead.

## Reflection

- Which spatial concept was hardest to internalize?
- How has your understanding of spatial data changed?

## Common Misconceptions

- “Spatial thinking is just GIS”
- “Tools guarantee correctness”
- “Maps are objective”
